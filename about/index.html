Nice to meet you!

I'm Tyler, a recent graduate from the University of Pittsburgh.

After exploring many subjects in college, I have realized that design is my passion. Since then, I have been working tirelessly to develop and improve my design skills. With my work, I am passionate about improving the lives of others and am always reading, researching, and studying to improve my designs.

I am also people-oriented with a focus on equality, social justice, diversity, and maintaining accessible design standards. As designers, we have the power and responsibility to make sure that everyone has access to what we create.

Let's make something great together!

If you would like to get in touch or just want to chat, please send me an email at olintyler@gmail.com or reach out to me on my LinkedIn. Feel free to also check out my Github account!
