Spot On

Role:

UX Designer

Class:

INFSCI 0410

Timeline:

March 2021 - April 2021

Tools used:

Figma

Project Context

This project was the final project for my Human-Centered Systems class. For the project, we went through every step of the design process to create a fully functional prototype of a new product.

Role

This project was created with a team of 7 students including myself, with the work being split up between research, requirements gathering, prototyping, and evaluating.

Challenge

Travellers are often stressed about making the most out of their vacation and finding the best activities to do while away.

Solution

We created SpotOn, an application that learns from travelers preferences to suggest an itinerary, reducing their cognitive burden while on vacation.
