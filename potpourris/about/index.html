About these projects

Here is a collection of all of the projects I have worked on in my class titled “Projects in English Composition: Nightmares and Daydreams.” This class had a central theme of nightmares and daydreams, which all of my projects were loosely based around. The projects were split by their medium. First was a mini-podcast that featured music, home-made sound effects, narration, and scriptwriting. For this, I chose to talk about how certain businesses and institutions capitalize on climate change. The second project was making an essay film, a genre that has been exploding in popularity on places like YouTube. This project was very involved with a ton of recording, editing, writing, and revising. For this project I discussed misinformation on the internet and why it is important in 2020. Lastly, we worked on a Photoshop archive remix project. This project took two or more old archival images from the University of Pittsburgh Libraries Archives and Special Collections department and remixed them into something new. Similar to my minicast, I chose to talk about climate change, making a cold-war style poster contrasting a green future with the one we are currently headed towards. Feel free to explore these projects and let me know if you would like to learn more about them.
